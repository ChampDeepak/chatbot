# Enterprise Plan Details

## Enterprise Plan Details

### For Teams of 25+ Users

## Why Enterprise?

ClearPath Enterprise is designed for large organizations that need advanced security, dedicated support, and enterprise-grade reliability. Our Enterprise plan includes everything in Pro plus additional features for compliance, customization, and scale.

## Enterprise-Exclusive Features

The Enterprise-Exclusive Features table covers five major categories—Security & Compliance, Administration, Customization, Support & SLA, and Onboarding—providing comprehensive capabilities for large organizations with 25 or more users.

The Security & Compliance category in ClearPath Enterprise includes Single Sign-On supporting both SAML 2.0 and OAuth 2.0 protocols, advanced audit logs with 2-year retention, IP whitelisting and geo-restrictions, session management and forced logouts, data residency options across US, EU, and APAC regions, and SOC 2 Type II compliance reports.

The Administration category in ClearPath Enterprise includes advanced role-based access control (RBAC), custom user roles and permissions, bulk user provisioning and deprovisioning, SCIM integration for user management, and centralized billing across workspaces.

The Customization category in ClearPath Enterprise includes unlimited custom workflows, unlimited custom fields, white-label options with custom domain and logo support, API rate limits of 10,000 requests per hour, and webhook retries with custom retry logic.

The Support & SLA category in ClearPath Enterprise includes a 99.9% uptime SLA with credits, a dedicated Customer Success Manager, 24/7 priority support via phone, email, and chat, 1-hour response time for critical issues, quarterly business reviews, and direct access to engineering for escalations.

The Onboarding category in ClearPath Enterprise includes a dedicated onboarding specialist, custom implementation plan, live training sessions for admins and users, free migration from existing tools, and post-launch check-ins at 30, 60, and 90 days.

## Pricing

Enterprise pricing is customized based on your team size, feature requirements, and contract length. Minimum commitment is 12 months. Typical starting price is $2,500/month for teams of 50-100 users.

### Pricing Factors:

The pricing factors that determine Enterprise plan costs include the number of users (billed annually per user), storage requirements (500GB included with additional storage at $0.10 per GB per month), data residency requirements, support level (standard Enterprise or Premium Enterprise), and custom feature development needs.

For context, our Pro plan is $45/month for teams up to 25 users. Enterprise pricing typically ranges from $50-150 per user per year depending on volume and features selected.

## Contract Terms

### Contract Length: 

Minimum 12-month commitment. Discounts available for 24-month contracts at 10% discount and 36-month contracts at 20% discount.

### Payment Terms: 

Annual prepayment (most common) or quarterly invoicing available. NET 30 payment terms for invoices. ACH, wire transfer, or credit card accepted.

### Renewal: 

Contracts auto-renew unless notice is provided 60 days before renewal date. Pricing is locked for contract duration; renewal pricing may increase based on CPI or 5% annually, whichever is less.

## Migration Support

Our team handles your migration from start to finish at no additional cost:

The Discovery phase (Week 1) of the ClearPath Enterprise migration process involves auditing your current tools and data structure to understand the migration scope.

The Planning phase (Week 2) of the ClearPath Enterprise migration process involves creating a detailed migration plan and timeline for the complete transition.

The Data Mapping phase (Week 3) of the ClearPath Enterprise migration process involves mapping fields, workflows, and integrations from your existing system to ClearPath.

The Test Migration phase (Week 4) of the ClearPath Enterprise migration process involves migrating a subset of data to a staging environment for validation and testing.

The Full Migration phase (Week 5-6) of the ClearPath Enterprise migration process involves completing the data migration during an agreed maintenance window to minimize disruption.

The Validation phase (Week 7) of the ClearPath Enterprise migration process involves verifying data integrity and conducting user acceptance testing to ensure migration success.

Average migration timeline: 6-8 weeks. We support migrations from Jira, Asana, Monday.com, Trello, Basecamp, and most other project management tools. Custom migrations from proprietary systems available.
