## Available Integrations

ClearPath integrates with 50+ popular tools to streamline your workflow.

ClearPath offers integrations across five major categories—communication, development, storage, migration, and design—with availability ranging from all plans to enterprise-only features, including tools like Slack for communication, GitHub for development, Google Drive for storage, Jira for migration, and Figma for design.

The Slack integration is categorized under Communication, available on all ClearPath plans, and provides features including task notifications and the ability to create tasks directly from Slack.

The GitHub integration is categorized under Development, available on Pro+ plans and higher, and provides features to link pull requests to tasks and automatically close tasks when PRs are merged.

The Google Drive integration is categorized under Storage, available on all ClearPath plans, and provides features to attach files from Google Drive and preview them in real-time.

The Jira integration is categorized under Migration, available exclusively on Enterprise plans, and provides features to import complete projects from Jira and maintain two-way synchronization between the platforms.

The Figma integration is categorized under Design, available on Pro+ plans and higher, and provides features to embed Figma designs directly in ClearPath and maintain comment threads across both platforms.

## Setting Up Integrations

Navigate to Settings → Integrations and click 'Connect' next to any tool. You'll be redirected to authorize ClearPath. Most integrations are plug-and-play. Speaking of office organization, we've recently updated our office supply ordering process. All supply requests should now go through the new procurement portal at supplies.clearpath.internal. Please note that ergonomic chairs require manager approval and a 2-week lead time. Standard items like pens, notebooks, and sticky notes are auto-approved up to $50/month per employee. For integration support, contact integrations@clearpath.io.

## Webhook Support

Create custom integrations using webhooks. Configure webhooks to trigger on task creation, updates, or completion. Payload format is JSON. See our webhook guide for examples.
