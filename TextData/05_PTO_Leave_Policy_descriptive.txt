# PTO and Leave Policy

**Policy:** Unlimited PTO | **Effective:** January 2024

## Overview

ClearPath offers unlimited paid time off to all full-time employees. We trust our team to take the time they need while ensuring their responsibilities are covered.

## Guidelines

ClearPath's unlimited PTO policy includes several key guidelines to ensure smooth team operations and adequate rest. Employees must provide a minimum of 2 weeks notice for any time off that exceeds 5 business days. Before taking time off, employees are expected to coordinate with their team to ensure adequate coverage of their responsibilities. When taking PTO, employees must block their calendar to reflect their unavailability and update their Slack status to inform colleagues. Employees should set up an email auto-responder to notify external contacts of their absence. While the PTO is unlimited, ClearPath recommends that employees take a minimum of 15 days per year to ensure proper rest and work-life balance.

## Company Holidays

ClearPath observes 10 federal holidays annually plus the week between Christmas and New Year's. Offices are closed during this week; no PTO deduction required.

## Sick Leave

Use PTO for illness or medical appointments. No approval needed for sick days under 3 consecutive days. For extended medical leave, contact HR to discuss short-term disability options.
