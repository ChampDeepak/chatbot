# Code of Conduct

Version: 3.1 | Effective: January 1, 2024

## Our Values

ClearPath is committed to fostering an inclusive, respectful workplace where everyone can thrive. We value diversity, transparency, and collaboration.

## Expected Behavior

ClearPath expects all employees to treat all colleagues, customers, and partners with respect in every interaction. Employees are expected to communicate professionally and constructively in all written and verbal communications. All ClearPath employees must honor their commitments and meet established deadlines for projects and deliverables. Employees are required to protect confidential information and maintain appropriate data security practices. All employees must report concerns through appropriate channels including management, HR, or the anonymous hotline.

## Unacceptable Behavior

ClearPath has zero tolerance for harassment, discrimination, intimidation, or retaliation. This includes inappropriate comments, unwelcome advances, or creating a hostile work environment. Violations may result in immediate termination.

## Reporting Violations

Report concerns to your manager, HR (hr@clearpath.io), or our anonymous hotline (1-800-555-0199). All reports are investigated promptly and confidentially.
