# Product Roadmap 2024

## Q1 2024 (Jan-Mar)

The Q1 2024 product roadmap includes five major feature releases focused on permissions, integrations, customization, and mobile improvements, with the advanced permissions system being a key enterprise-focused enhancement.

In Q1 2024 (January through March), the product will launch an advanced permissions system that provides granular role controls for fine-tuned access management.

In Q1 2024 (January through March), the product will introduce time tracking integration to enable users to monitor and record time spent on tasks directly within the platform.

In Q1 2024 (January through March), the product will deliver custom dashboards that allow users to create personalized views and metrics tailored to their specific needs.

In Q1 2024 (January through March), the product will release mobile app offline mode improvements to enhance functionality and reliability when users are working without an internet connection.

In Q1 2024 (January through March), the product will launch Slack app v2 with interactive messages, enabling richer two-way communication and actions directly within Slack conversations.

## Q2 2024 (Apr-Jun)

The Q2 2024 product roadmap includes five major feature releases focused on resource planning, executive views, automation, Microsoft integration, and enterprise API capabilities, with resource management being the headline feature for this quarter.

In Q2 2024 (April through June), the product will introduce resource management and capacity planning features to help teams allocate personnel and track availability across projects.

In Q2 2024 (April through June), the product will launch a portfolio view for executives that provides high-level visibility and strategic oversight across multiple projects and initiatives.

In Q2 2024 (April through June), the product will deliver advanced automation rules that enable users to create sophisticated conditional workflows and triggers to streamline repetitive processes.

In Q2 2024 (April through June), the product will release Microsoft Teams integration to bring project management capabilities directly into the Microsoft Teams collaboration environment.

In Q2 2024 (April through June), the product will implement enhanced API rate limits for Enterprise customers, providing higher throughput and capacity for integration and automation needs.

## Q3 2024 (Jul-Sep)

The Q3 2024 product roadmap includes five major feature releases focused on artificial intelligence, search, templates, workspace architecture, and data export capabilities, with AI-powered task suggestions being the most significant innovation for this quarter.

In Q3 2024 (July through September), the product will launch AI-powered task suggestions and estimates that use machine learning to recommend next steps and predict effort required for completing work.

In Q3 2024 (July through September), the product will deliver global search improvements that enhance the speed, accuracy, and relevance of finding information across all projects and workspaces.

In Q3 2024 (July through September), the product will introduce a template marketplace where users can discover, share, and utilize pre-built project templates created by the community and the company.

In Q3 2024 (July through September), the product will release multi-workspace support that allows users to work across multiple separate organizational environments within a single account.

In Q3 2024 (July through September), the product will provide advanced export formats that enable users to extract their data in a wider variety of file types and structures for reporting and analysis purposes.

## Q4 2024 (Oct-Dec)

The Q4 2024 product roadmap includes five major feature releases focused on goal tracking, real-time collaboration, video communication, performance optimization, and year-end analytics, with goals and OKR tracking representing a major strategic planning capability.

In Q4 2024 (October through December), the product will launch goals and OKR tracking functionality that enables teams to set, monitor, and measure progress toward objectives and key results.

In Q4 2024 (October through December), the product will introduce real-time collaboration in a Google Docs style, allowing multiple users to simultaneously edit and see changes instantly without page refreshes.

In Q4 2024 (October through December), the product will deliver video messaging integration that enables users to record and share video messages directly within the platform for asynchronous communication.

In Q4 2024 (October through December), the product will implement performance improvements for large projects to ensure the system remains fast and responsive even when managing complex initiatives with extensive data.

In Q4 2024 (October through December), the product will release year-end reporting features that provide specialized analytics and summaries designed for annual reviews and strategic planning cycles.
